In the proposed Red2Green pipeline, image compression is performed at the client side to reduce network overhead and improve upload latency before data reaches the cloud backend. After capture/selection, the mobile application decodes the image, constrains the longest edge to 1080 pixels while preserving aspect ratio, and re-encodes the frame in JPEG format at approximately 80% quality; the compressed file is then uploaded to Cloudinary as the reporting artifact. This pre-upload optimization minimizes bandwidth consumption, lowers transmission time on unstable mobile links, and decreases storage cost, while maintaining sufficient visual fidelity for issue verification and NGO-led resolution workflows. The method is lightweight, deterministic, and suitable for real-time civic reporting scenarios where responsiveness and resource efficiency are critical.
